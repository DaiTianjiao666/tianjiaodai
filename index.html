    Hello, I am Zihan Luo, a CS Ph.D. candidate at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST). I am fortunate to be supervised by Professor Hong Huang and Senior Researcher Jianxun Lian. Before that, I received my Bachelor degree in Electronic Engineering from HUST in 2020, and was fortunate to be advised by Professor Rui Yin from 2019-2020.

    My research interests include graph representation learning and Large Language Models (LLM), especially on topics like fairness and bias. I have published several papers at the top international AI conferences and journals with total Google Scholar citations 20+.
